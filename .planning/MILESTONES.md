## v1.0 MVP

**Delivered:** 2026-01-17
**Timeline:** 2 days (2026-01-16 to 2026-01-17)
**Git range:** `feat(01-01)` to `feat(04-04)`
**Tag:** v1.0

### Summary

Local-first emotional companion with on-device LLM, persistent memory, and crisis safety features. App ready for TestFlight beta testing.

### Key Accomplishments

1. **On-device LLM infrastructure** — Model downloads with pause/resume, initializes with memory pressure handling
2. **Chat experience with safety** — Streaming responses, voice input, crisis detection with hotline resources
3. **Memory system** — Conversation extraction, decay calculations, retrieval, and injection into prompts
4. **TestFlight distribution** — Custom icon, splash screen, onboarding, and settings ready for beta testers
